  • Prep

    Before diving in, we'll review your current social media presence (if any) and discuss your brand goals. You'll receive our Social Media Strategy Questionnaire to help us understand your target audience, brand voice, and growth objectives. This will be the groundwork for developing a tailored strategy.

  • Day 01.

    We'll kick off with a strategy session to outline your content themes, posting frequency, and key goals. Our team will develop a social media calendar for the month, ensuring a cohesive and strategic approach to your online presence.

  • Day 02.

    We’ll start creating customized content for your brand. This includes post graphics, engaging captions, hashtags, and stories tailored to your target audience. You’ll review and approve the content before we proceed with scheduling.

  • Day 03.

    With the content approved, we’ll schedule and publish posts across your social media platforms. Our team will actively engage with your followers, responding to comments and messages, while also monitoring the performance of each post to ensure maximum reach.

  • Day 04.

    We'll track the performance of your social media efforts throughout the week. Based on the analytics, we’ll fine-tune the strategy, making necessary adjustments to improve engagement and reach.

  • Day 05.

    At the end of the month, we’ll provide you with a detailed report on your social media performance. We'll review key metrics, including engagement rates, follower growth, and conversions, and then plan the content and strategy for the following month.
